# BRAND AND VOICE — Zone Two Health and Performance

## The thesis (read this first)

The name is the strategy. "Zone two" is the aerobic base-building zone: the steady, sustainable, unglamorous work that lets an athlete go far and keep going. That idea is the whole brand. This is not a crack-your-back pain clinic. It is a clinic for people who want to move well and keep moving, built on the same values as the technical-outdoor world the design references come from: patient, durable, performance over quick fixes.

Everything ladders up to one arc:

**Recover. Rebuild. Perform.**

Post-operative rehab and dry needling help you recover. Chiropractic and rehab help you rebuild. Exercise physiology helps you perform. Zone two is the long, patient middle that connects them.

## Audience

Active adults in and around Eastwood who take their bodies seriously: runners, hikers, lifters, weekend skiers, people coming back from surgery who want to return to their sport, and people who would rather build a base than chase a quick fix. They respond to competence and calm, not hype.

## Brand pillars

1. **Base over band-aids.** The valuable work is consistent and foundational.
2. **Technical and evidence-led.** Specifics, not promises.
3. **Movement is the point.** Framed around capability and getting back to what you love.
4. **Quiet confidence.** No shouting, no superlatives.

## Voice and tone

Calm, technical, physically specific. Confident without hype. The coach who is also an endurance nerd. Short declarative sentences. Strong physical verbs. Fragments are fine in display type. Almost no exclamation marks.

**We sound like:** "Your first visit is 45 minutes. A full movement assessment, a conversation about your goals, and a plan for what comes next."

**We do not sound like:** "Unlock your body's full potential and say goodbye to pain forever!" (Hype, a banned outcome claim, and it would breach the advertising rules.)

### Lexicon

Words and ideas we lean on: base, build, season, distance, return, movement, assessment, programme, capacity, load, recovery, rebuild, perform, steady, durable, foundation.

Words we never use: see the banned list in `CLAUDE.md`, plus anything that promises an outcome (see `COMPLIANCE.md`): pain-free, cure, fix, heal you, guaranteed, instant, best, number one.

### Formatting rules for copy

- Australian spelling.
- No em dashes. Ever.
- Headlines: two to five words, often split across two lines, imperative or physical.
- Eyebrows: short, uppercase, letter-spaced, often in mono type (see `DESIGN-SYSTEM.md`). Good for service labels and the location coordinate motif.

## The method name

Propose **"The Zone Two Method"** as the named, numbered approach (the structural equivalent of Myodetox's method, but with compliant, non-outcome language). Suggested steps, all factual and process-based, none promising a result:

1. **Assess.** A full look at how you move and where load is going.
2. **Plan.** A programme built around your goals and your history.
3. **Treat.** Hands-on care and the right modalities for you.
4. **Load.** Structured exercise to build capacity over time.
5. **Progress.** We adjust as you go and track the work, not the promises.
